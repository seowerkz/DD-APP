Accident Report

Call Double D

  • Notify dispatch or someone in the Double D office IMMEDIATELY.

Secure the Scene

  • Stop; turn on your emergency flashers and shut down your vehicle; do NOT move your truck until the police arrive.
  • Set out warning devices; protect the scene. Assist the injured but do not move anyone; wait for medical assistance.

Notify the Authorities

  • Call the police; request medical assistance if needed.
  • Call Great West: (800) 228-8040
  • Stay at the scene. Be polite, courteous, and professional. Do NOT admit guilt or apologize.

Document the Accident

  • Find and begin filling out the Great West Accident Report in your truck.
  • Follow the Great West form for further instructions, call dispatch for questions.
